I currently integrate scalable, grown graphene crystals into optoelectronic devices aimed at next‑generation telecommunications applications.

I have experience in device microfabrication, thin‑film deposition through wet‑chemistry methods, and the physical characterization of these systems.

My research interests include the experimental investigation of novel phenomena in van der Waals heterostructures based on two‑dimensional materials, as well as the surface properties of molecular assemblies at the nanoscale and in thin films.
